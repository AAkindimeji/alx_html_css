How does this work?

A freemium package operates on a model where users are provided with a basic, free version of a product or service, often with limited features or functionality. This serves as an entry point for users to experience the offering without any monetary commitment. The goal is to entice users with the value and utility of the free version, encouraging them to later upgrade to a premium, paid version that offers additional features or enhanced capabilities.
The freemium approach leverages the principle that users, once familiar with and deriving value from the free version, may find it worthwhile to invest in the premium version to access more advanced features or a fuller set of services. This dual-tiered strategy enables businesses to broaden their user base by attracting a larger audience with the free offering while generating revenue from those who choose to upgrade for a more comprehensive experience.
